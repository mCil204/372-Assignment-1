Travelogue: Phuket

Overview

Phuket is Thailand's largest island and a popular tourist destination. Phuket offers something for every kind of traveler from beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and delicous Thai cuisine. The island itself is naturally charming and it meshes together well with its rich cultural heritage which features Buddhist temples, fresh markets, and exciting stadiums.

One of Phuket's most famous beaches, Patong beach

Must-See Spots

  1. Patong Beach
  2. Patong Boxing Stadium
  3. Big Buddha
  4. Wat Chalong
  5. Phuket FantaSea

Visitor Tips

  • Visit during the dry season from December to March
  • The food can get pretty spicy, so be careful about what you get
  • When visiting local temples, it is best to dress modestly, remove your shoes, and be respectful
  • Rent scooters for ease of travel
